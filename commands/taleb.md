**Fragility / Robustness / Antifragility** — Does the thinking assume a stable world? Does it break under disorder, survive it, or gain from it? Is there a barbell or is the author all-in on one scenario?

**Skin in the Game** — Is the author exposed to the downside if they're wrong? Are they an observer giving advice without bearing consequences?

**Via Negativa** — Is the author adding complexity to solve a problem that subtraction would fix?

**IYI Pattern** — Is this the reasoning of someone educated beyond their judgment? Do they cite credentials, complexity, or consensus as argument?
